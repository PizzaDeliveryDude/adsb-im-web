What is Tailscale?

Tailscale is one of several "free for personal use" site-to-site networking services (also known as VPN, Virtual Local Network and other confusing terms). Essentially, it allows you to connect multiple computers that can talk to each other as if they were on the same LAN, even if they are not and are in fact thousands of kilometers apart. This works through typical home network setups without any configuration changes and on most public networks. Corporate networks may have controls in place that prevent services like this from working.

How do I set this up?

First, this is not sponsored content. I get nothing for linking to them, none of the links here are affiliate links (I don't even know if they have that). This is simply a service that is based on open source technology, run by people I trust reasonably well, and it's super easy for non-techies to get going.
With that out of the way, go to the Tailscale website and create an account (that's the "Get Started" button). This is easiest if you simply link to your existing Gmail, GitHub, or Apple ID.
Once that is done, you'll be at a page where it asks you to set up your first system. BEFORE you do that, very important, go to their Billing Plans page, ignore all the nice big options to give them money, and at the bottom center click on "Choose our personal plan". This will get you a popup to acknowledge the limitation. Be warned, if you want to deploy more than 99 feeders, this may be too restrictive ðŸ¤£.
Now go back to Machines and click on "Add Device". This will allow you to set up Tailscale on the computer that you are using to connect to the website. I recommend NOT doing this on your phone - that is possible, but a bit... painful to manage things with... a Windows / Mac / Linux system is the much better choice.

How do I connect my {% if 'adsb.im' in x_forwarded_host %}ADSB.im{% else %}SDRE.im{% endif %} feeder?

On the feeder homepage, select "Management" und der "System" menu item. There at the bottom you'll find the option to add this feeder to a tailnet (the network you created in the previous step). Click on the "Submit" button in the Add Tailscale section. This will reload the page and (once you scroll down again) present you with a link to log in. Click the link, sign in with the same account, and click on "Connect".
That's it, you should be done.
